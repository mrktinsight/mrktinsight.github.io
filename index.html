About

MRKT Insight, LLC is an independent app development studio focused on building thoughtful, well-crafted Android applications. We create apps across a range of interests — from music and entertainment to productivity and beyond — and publish them on Google Play.

Based in St. Charles, Missouri, MRKT Insight is a small studio that values clean design, solid engineering, and respect for the communities our apps serve.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 27, 2026

MRKT Insight, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates mobile applications published on Google Play. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our apps.

Information We Collect

Our apps are designed to respect your privacy. We do not require account creation, and we do not collect, store, or transmit personal information such as your name, email address, or location.

Usage Data

Our apps may use standard Android platform services and third-party services that collect limited technical data, including:

  • Device type, operating system version, and app version (for compatibility and crash reporting)
  • Anonymous usage statistics provided by Google Play (such as install counts and crash logs)

This data is collected by the Android platform and Google Play, not directly by us. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details on how Google handles this data.

Network Access

Our apps require internet access to stream audio content from third-party sources such as the Internet Archive. We do not intercept, log, or store your network activity or the content you access.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may link to or stream content from third-party services (such as the Internet Archive). These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
